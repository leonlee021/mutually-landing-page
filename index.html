How It Works

1. Request a task

Simply enter what you need help with, and submit it to our community.

2. Wait for taskers to accept

Taskers will see your request and those interested will respond.

3. Chat with potential taskers

Discuss the task details with taskers and agree on terms.

4. Task completion

Once the task is done, mark it complete and leave feedback.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mutually?

Mutually is an app that allows neighbors to help each other with small, daily tasks. Whether you need help with deliveries, moving, or something else, Mutually connects you with people nearby.

How do I request a task?

You can request a task by simply entering what you need help with and submitting it to our community through the app. Helpers nearby can then accept your request.

Is it free to request a task?

While some tasks may be done for free by helpful neighbors, you can also offer compensation for more challenging or time-consuming tasks.

How do I become a helper?

Becoming a helper is easy! Once you sign up, you can browse available tasks in your area and choose the ones you'd like to help with.
