How It Works

Requester

1. Post a task request

This will send a notification to all available taskers in your area!

Tasker

1. Accept the task

If you are available and in the area, accept the task to offer your services!

Requester

2. Chat with taskers

Discuss the details with all taskers who have accepted your task, and choose one when ready to finalize the agreement.

Tasker

2. Complete the task

If you have been chosen as the tasker, complete the task!

Requester

3. Mark the task as complete

Once the task is completed, mark it as such to send the payment. Afterwards, you can leave feedback for the tasker!
